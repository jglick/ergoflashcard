Lao Basic Course: Greeting Cycle
sábaaj - to be well, comfortable
sábaajdii - good, well
bɔɔ - [question particle]
sábaajdii bɔɔ - How are you, how are things?
càw - you
càw sábaajdii bɔɔ - How are you?
khɔ̂ɔpcaj - thank you
sábaajdii khɔ̂ɔpcaj - Fine, thank you.
